Warrior Cats

Warrior Cats is a book series by Erin Hunter. It is about a forest of cats in four Cslans

ThunderClan

ThunderClan's camp is in the forest

WindClan

WindClan lives on the moor, and are normally lean cats. They mostly hunt rabbits

RiverClan

RiverClan is the only Clan among the four that swims and eats fish. Their camp

ShadowClan

As stated in their name, ShadowClan is a very suspicious Clan.

SkyClan

SkyClan is a Clan

StarClan

StarClan is where every warrior goes where they die. It is the Warrior Cats world equivalent of heaven.

The Dark Forest

The Dark Forest is the place where evil cats/cats who do not deserve the luxury of StarClan go after death. It is the Warrior Cats world equivalent to hell.
