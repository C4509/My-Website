Warrior Cats

Warrior Cats is a book series by Erin Hunter. It is about a forest of cats in four Clans

ThunderClan

ThunderClan's camp is in the forest, in primarily deciduous woodland.

WindClan

WindClan lives on the moor, and are normally lean cats. They mostly hunt rabbits.

RiverClan

RiverClan is the only Clan among the four that swims and eats fish. Their camp is among reeds and ferns.

ShadowClan

As stated in their name, ShadowClan is a very sneaky Clan. They have good hiding skills and they live among pine trees.

SkyClan

SkyClan is a Clan that was driven out of the forest a long time ago, but now has been restored and lives at the lake with the other four Clans. Their special ability is jumping.

StarClan

StarClan is where every warrior goes where they die. It is the Warrior Cats world equivalent of heaven.

The Dark Forest

The Dark Forest is the place where evil cats/cats who do not deserve the luxury of StarClan go after death. It is the Warrior Cats world equivalent to hell.
